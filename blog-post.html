Fish diet up to -5 kg per week! Menu for 7 days!

Fish diet is quite satisfying, so it is easy to tolerate, provided, of course, that you love fish. Fish is a very valuable dietary product for several reasons: it consists of protein, which is easily digested by our body, contains a lot of proteins that do not allow the muscle mass to melt, fish is less calorific than meat, and also contains less fat. In addition, the fatty acids that are found in fish accelerate the metabolism, and contribute to weight loss.

This menu allows you to quickly weight loss due to a combination of several factors:

Products are selected according to the canons of separate nutrition, only combining with each other, which are quickly and well absorbed, and do not slur the body. After 2-3 days of such a diet, you will feel light in the stomach, and at the same time will not feel weak from malnutrition!

The diet can be observed as 1 day, or 2-3 weeks, or more. On this diet, you can lose from 5 kilograms of weight. Fish can be prepared in any way except frying. You can add spices to taste, the main thing is to make sure that the spices are not included in the composition salt came in.

Why the Dukan diet is dangerous for your health

Why the Dukan diet is dangerous for your health

Recently, the diet of dietitian-nutritionist Pierre Dukan is gaining popularity. More than 11 million books on the protein diet have been sold worldwide. A clear eating algorithm, lack of hunger, and a motivating book seem like a good idea. a panacea for people who want to lose weight. But if only it were that simple!

The essence of the diet of a French dietitian is "to eat and lose weight", without limiting yourself in the size of portions and the number of meals. At the same time, as Dukan writes, his method is not a diet, but a lifestyle. He suggests starting with an "attack" on which the thinner can eat only low-fat protein foods. Then the longest stage – alternating: one day eat "protein", the other - "protein" and vegetables. After that, the consolidation, where you slowly return to the diet of an ordinary person, starting to include "non-protein" products in your diet. Finally, stabilization is a step that helps a person hold the weight for the rest of his life. You can eat anything you want, but once a week, always on Thursday, arrange a protein day for yourself.

The results shown by Dukan's followers are truly astounding. However, along with the extra pounds, something more weighty can go-yours health.

Monotonous nutrition and protein in large quantities are nonphysiological, and therefore can be harmful to both a healthy person and a patient with pathological diseases. Problems can occur from the heart, liver, kidneys and gastrointestinal tract.

Gastro-intestinal tract

The Association of dietitians of the United States attributed the protein diet of Dukan to the number of life-threatening.

The nutrition system that the dietitian calls for is poor in fat and carbohydrates — the main elements that improve the functioning of the intestines. In conditions of delayed digestion, there is a development of stagnant phenomena and rapid reproduction of microorganisms.

Protein food in the case of intestinal pathology associated with impaired motor skills, begins to rot, which leads to intoxication and non-assimilation of protein. Sometimes this happens in healthy people – protein food makes it worse and provokes.

As a result, the person is accompanied by poor health, bad breath, nausea, dizziness, flatulence, diarrhea and constipation, which only aggravate the intoxication of the body.

Menstruation

A protein diet involves a limited intake of fat, which is necessary for the female body. Because of this, many women who are sitting on proteins lose their normal and normal function. the cycle is the result of hormonal imbalance. This is accompanied by a deterioration in the condition of hair, nails,dry mucous and skin. In the future, there is a decrease in libido, impaired fertility, and polycystic ovarian syndrome.

The kidneys

It is known that diets with a high protein content are contraindicated patients with chronic kidney disease. However, it is worth noting that after the age of 50, age-related decline in liver and kidney function begins, which is not a pathology, but the age norm. Accordingly, older people should be more careful when choosing this type of diet.

At the same time, short courses eating exclusively protein-rich foods is considered harmless. Therefore, the popular Dukan diet can be replaced with a more comfortable for the body – protein-carbohydrate alternation.

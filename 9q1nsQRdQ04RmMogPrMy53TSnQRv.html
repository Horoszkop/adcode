Deduction for treatment of the child.

If you and your child (up to 18 years old or up to 24 years old in full-time training) visited doctors for a fee, received treatment, passed tests, bought medicines on an official letterhead from a doctor, or had an operation. Then 13% of the you can return the money you spent. You can return it for the past 3 years. If it is 2019, then by the end of December hurry up to return for 2016, and you can also return for 2017 and 2018. For 2019, it will be possible to submit a Declaration in 2020.

There are mandatory conditions:

1. At least one of the child's parents must officially work and pay 13% personal income tax from the salary (paid by the employer)

2. you spend a year collecting receipts for treatment and official forms for medicines (be sure to tell at the reception what you need for the tax service, otherwise they will write on simple pieces of paper). You copy all the receipts, because they fade. Keep everything safe in the folder to quickly find it. There is a separate folder for each family member and for each year.

3. The medical facility must be licensed.

4. on the basis of receipts in medical institutions (there may be many of them), you are given a certificate for the tax service about your expenses. Ask the parent who receives the official certificate to write it out wages and pays 13% of personal income tax. Also at work, take the 2NDFL certificate of your salary.

5. in the personal account of the tax authority of the parent for whom all the certificates are issued, we fill out a Declaration. I fill it out for all my family members. You can log in to your merchant profile using public services. Attach scans to the Declaration all certificates and personal income tax, a scan of the child's birth certificate. And there you can immediately fill out an application for a refund, indicating the details of the parent for whom the Declaration was filled out.

After filling out and sending the Declaration, the tax service checks it for 3 months, then transfers the money within 1 month. Don't delay after sending the Declaration, you will have to wait for money for 4 months.

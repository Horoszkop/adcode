6 things that will help you lose weight

6 things that will help you lose weight

Of course, everyone dreams of a magic pill that will easily tighten all the sagging and add volume only where you want it.

But unfortunately, the world of science is now occupied with other, more important issues.

So I'll tell you about SIX already long-ago invented and tested tools that REALLY WORK in the field of weight LOSS

1. Inspiration 2. Motivation 3. Intent

4. Discipline 5. Habit 6. Passion

How's that does it work?

Inspiration is a feeling that pushes you to do something.

  • Inspiration can be positive:

Someone's living example of transformation and your desire to get a similar result

  • Or it can be negative:

For example, you saw your the last photo and realized how far you are from your best form

Psychology confirms by its research thatThe FORMER works better. Clings to the human huge and selfish Self, which simply cannot allow SOMEONE to be successfully

People should (and most likely will) change because they want to, not because someone wants them to change.

Inspiration is the initiator of making decisions about change.

At first, it is a strong, emotional stimulant of commitment. an action plan ("it's Decided I'm losing weight"), but it doesn't work for a very long time.

Resources of inspiration alone are not enough for such a long journey as saying goodbye to excess pounds

And that's why there is MOTIVATION in the arena

Motivation drives you to achieve your goal and is strong an incentive to follow the diet regime in the first weeks of the diet.

The desire to simply lose weight can serve as inspiration, but the MOTIVATION can only be a concrete and REAL reference point.

You must define for yourself what it means to you SLIM.

The best option is an item of clothing that you plan to fit into after a diet, or maybe a chic photo shoot or vacation on the beach, where you can appear on the beach in a chic swimsuit without the slightest hesitation.

Motivation is a powerful energy, but it can noticeably sink, especially when your vital energy is reduced due to high loads and insufficient nutrition (we are still talking about a diet).

Once a goal is set, daily or weekly reminders of what you want from your diet can go a long way help maintain motivation in the first weeks.
This may include a written reminder taped to the refrigerator, a fitting of clothes that you plan to "fit in" with new dimensions, or it may be a weekly collage of your photos.

But if inspiration and motivation are a kind of push to action.

What motivates you to take these actions? To bring them to life?

And this is the INTENTION

Motivation works by giving you a clear goal, but just setting a goal doesn't lead to results.

It's like deciding to build an IKEA wardrobe. But the solution alone will not bring the Cabinet together. You must open the instructions and follow certain algorithms .

Intent is the application of inspiration and motivation to create a plan.

Going back to the closet, you can associate this plan with the requirements of the build instructions that you must follow to achieve your goal. You can't skip a couple of items or start collecting from the last one.
If you have all the motivation in the world, but no plan or intention to follow the plan, you will not have results'.

Because intent supports commitment to a plan , the first and most important action you can take to support intent is to develop a plan.

It's nice to think about losing 8 kg over the next 3 months, but in order to do this, it is necessary to determine exactly what dishes you will eat tomorrow and the next day, what foods will form the basis of the diet and what nishtyki you will be able to write into your menu, what to enjoy them in the evenings after a hard working day.
You won't be able to follow the plan for two days, but for three - no.

Therefore, we will pay attention to such a companion as DISCIPLINE

Filling in the gaps between weakened intentions and low tides of motivation is a discipline .

Discipline is the use of willpower to achieve a goal.

Your willpower is limited, so you can't use it all the time, but when your motivation is temporarily low, it's what you need to tap into

This satellite involves following a plan, even if you may no longer be able to you don't want to do this.

Knowing that when you go on a diet, you will face times when you will want to finish can help you prepare for the discipline.

The expectation that things will get tough, and the expectation that you will have to be disciplined and sometimes use force willpower is one of the best ways to prevent the possibility of "moving out".

Because your willpower is limited, you can't constantly use discipline because of the risk of burnout.

Because discipline has a strong effect on your emotions. cognitive process.

At some point, when inspiration has already made you start, motivation has allowed you to define a goal, intent has helped you create a specific plan, and discipline has allowed you to follow it for a long time, your dietary practices will become a habit.

Weeks and months of keeping a certain diet (not 21 days, as many claim), form habits that make your daily diet easier.

After all, it is easier to follow a habit, even if you have low motivation.

To form habits, a good adherence should occur in the early stages of the diet (today I want to, and tomorrow I don't, because I'm in a bad mood â€“ it doesn't work).

Keep in mind that strict adherence to the diet will eventually help to some extent to get rid of the difficulties associated with improper nutrition, which is ALREADY it has become a habit due to long-term use.
This can help motivate you to follow your diet plan in the early stages.

The weeks immediately before habits Mature are one of the hardest stages of a diet.

To speed up the appearance and maturing habits, create YOUR own diet. Learn how to cook food and do it regularly, according to YOUR schedule and daily routine. It doesn't matter if it's daily or weekly. The main thing is that it helps to follow the plan.

  • Keep a regular daily routine: weekly train on the same days, and also Wake up and go to bed.
  • Have access to food sources that will help you satisfy your hunger and stay on track, even when you are on the move, away from home.
  • You have to understand what products fit easily into your diet without breaking the rules of the diet.

If you've been working on changing your body composition for long enough, you might even enjoy the process of dieting.

Support for a healthy diet, reduction of fat tissue and increase in muscle mass, and the very experience of how to control your appearance the look and feel of it can be addictive.

Many people love this process so much that they develop a real passion for it.

The manipulation of nutrients, calories, loads and the results of all these processes can be seen in the mirror.

Isn't that right a miracle?

Everything is in your hands.

The main thing is to set real goals and form feasible plans!

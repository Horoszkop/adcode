How do I convince my friends or family to invest? This question is often asked by those who have already started investing themselves and have experienced the charm of the investor's path. How do I convince you? Sometimes they even write to me: here is my friend, write to him and offer to start investing! Let him also manage his money correctly.

But this is the wrong approach. I believe that a person should reach the investment stage by himself. You can convince him. But if he is not ready , he may behave incorrectly: panic, to speculate - and as a result to lose and not to increase capital. Forget about the airbag and about diversification.

That's why I don't write to anyone myself. But if you are ready - you can ask for advice or develop an investment portfolio. The best thing you can do for your relatives or friends - it's giving them books to read about investing or watch videos. But you don't have to convince them.
